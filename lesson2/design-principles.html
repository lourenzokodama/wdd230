Hick's Law

BYU Pathway Worldwide

Visitors to this website have two options. It is either apply now or request information. They have two apply now buttons, which are located in the navigation bar and on the hero image. While the request info button is located beside the apply now button in the hero image. This website's visitors will not be perplexed when making a decision.

PARC: Alignment

FamilySearch

The principle of Alignment is evident in this website. First, the navigation bar items have centered vertical alignment. Next, the header items have the same vertical alignment. Then, the title, description, and buttons in the body have centered horizontal alignment.

White Space

Adobe

This site is visually appealing to me, because of the exceptional use of negative space. Because the texts are appropriately spaced out, it is easy to read. I can also distinguish between the "start free trial" button and the "learn more" link and avoid mistakenly touching one or the other due to their gaps.
